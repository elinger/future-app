Midžor (Serbian Cyrillic: Миџор, pronounced [mîdʒɔr], Bulgarian: Миджур, Midzhur) is a peak in the Balkan Mountains, situated on the border between Serbia and Bulgaria. At 2,169 metres (7,116 ft), it is the highest peak of the Western Balkan Mountains, as well as the highest of Serbia outside Kosovo.[note 1] Midžor is the 12th highest peak in the Balkan Mountains.
